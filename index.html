A special alloy has derived for high temperature application eg. in electric motor , car radiator and high temperature lamps.HMP is also ideal for prevention of reflows of the first connection where joins are made successively or are very dense.KTE has also developed alloys for low melting point .KTE's brazing division plays an important role in the air conditioning and refrigeration industry.KTE manufactures for both general as well as special application brazing alloys of high performance.



 Soldering fluxes

Flux is indispensable in the soldering of electronic components and other applications. A soldering flux performs many functions, the most important of which is the removal of oxides film, other tarnished film and surface film from the metal to be soldered .It exposes clean surface for optimum wetting and solder 'bond' formation.Our flux are classified into different categories which are listed with their properties.





 PCB chemicals

KTE also has developed a water soluble non resin flux which is called as solder paint.This type of paint is user friendly because of excellent wetting and good thixotropic properties.Post soldering flux residues generally are removed from assemblies by organic solvent.KTE's ultra cleaners have no CFS and are designed to remove both ionic and non ionic flux residues in order to maximize the reliability of electronic assemblies.





 Solder Paste

To meet the exact requirement of SMT component assembly, hybrid and chip bumping application,KTE has developed solder paste for the Indian electronic and electrical industry. Solder paste is a homogenous mixture of finely graded oxide free pre alloyes solder powder suspended in a thixotropic flux. Extremly rapid flow and increased wetting are obtained in 'bond' solder paste.

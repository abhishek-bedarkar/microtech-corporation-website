Training



Regular training of operators/supervisors & managers is mandatory as far as ISO is concerned. Even updating on recent developments in the field & remainder of many precautions & procedures is also necessary for the consistancy in quality & productivity. Training of employees is also heplful to refresh & enhance their liability towars the company.Considering above need Microtech Corporation took initiative in providing training facility to the customers even at their shopfloors.Experts who have long & valuable experience in the field of soldering are available for the training, guidance & also for "problems solving" too.

Training on soldering process is divided in three parts viz Hand Soldering Process, Wave Soldering Process & SMT Soldering Process. Each subject is handled in details including cost & wastage awareness.Awareness regarding safety during dross handling etc is also communicated properly.Audiovisual presentation, verbal explaination & practical demonstration are the ways in which the whole training is given to customer. Each customer may require specially designed package to suit their setup, products & educational level of the trainees.

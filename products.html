Lead free fluxes


Flux is indispensable in the soldering of electronic components and other application .A soldering flux performs many function ,the most important of which is the removal of oxides film,other tarnished film and surface film from the metal to be soldered.It exposes clean surface for optimum wetting and solder 'bond' formation.A flux's ability to prepare good surface for soldering is the measure of its activity.From our long and vast experience, we have constantly researched flux technology and have developed various types of fluxes,which provide unrivaled performance.Our fluxes are classified into different categories which are listed below with their properties and recommended application.

PCB lacquer

PCB Lacquer : It’s a post soldering lacquer which cures at room temperature within 15-20 minutes. Its gives transparent and resolderable coating. It protects the PCB from environmental hazards.

Copper Lite (copper cleaning chemicals)

Copperlite chemical can be used as PCB precleaner, which ensures the solderability of printed circuit boards by removing surface contaminations on the copper conductors. After that PCB can be preserved by rolltinning or lacquering. Copperlite solution is water based and a clear solution. It can work on silver surface also. The cleaning effect is almost instantaneous. After use, rinse the PCB in clean water and dry. Copperlite can be available in solid as well as liquid form named cp- 30.

Anti-oxident(power/metal/oil)

It’s a white colored amorphous powder used to deoxidize Tin from dross and send it back to solder bath. It also form a protective layer on the solder bath surface which cuts the contact of Oxygen with bath material and reduces further dross formation.


Glue

It’s a 200 gm bottle which contains glue required to stick SMD components to PCB for wave soldering process.
